FALCON9

Falcon 9 is a two-stage rocket designed and manufactured by SpaceX for the reliable and safe transport of satellites and Dragon spacecraft into orbit.

DRAGON SPACECRAFT

The Dragon capsule, also known as the pressurized section, allows for the transport of people as well as environmentally sensitive cargo. Capable of carrying humans in the ditant future.

COMPOSITE FAIRING

the payload fairing is for the delivery of the satellites to destination in low Earth orbit(LEO), geosynchronized transfer orbit(GTO) and beyond.

FIRST STAGE

Falcon 9's first stage incorporates nine Merlin engines and aluminium-lithium alloy tanks containing liquid oxygen and rocket grade kerosene (RP-1) propellent.

NINE MARLINE ENGINES

With its nine first-stage Merlin engines clustered together, Falcon9 can sustain up to two engine shutdowns during flights and still successfully complete its mission. Falcon9 is the only launch vehicle in its class with this feature.
